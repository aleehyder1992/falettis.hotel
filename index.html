Faletti's Hotel

The Faletti’s Hotel is a unique blend of old and new, having successfully preserved its past aura to provide its guests with a lifetime memory. It is a marvellous mélange of Victorian, Colonial and Art Deco styles in its interiors. The spacious guest rooms and sparkling marble floors are a constant reminder of the elegance of the colonial era. The “Greatest of Personalities” in our history have walked the corridors of the hotel and shared the most daring ideas over a cup of tea in its restaurant and lounges.
